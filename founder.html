Founder — Syed Asif Ali

Syed Asif Ali is a digital media creator, entrepreneur, and communications professional based in the UAE and Pakistan. He is the founder of Point Media and Pointika, with expertise in content creation, media visibility, and strategic storytelling.

Achievements & Milestones:

  • Founder of Point Media & Pointika
  • Specialized in digital brand growth & online presence
  • Managed social media for top influencers and public figures
  • Featured on TechBullion & other reputable media outlets
  • Strategic storytelling & media consultancy
  • Successful digital campaigns across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and LinkedIn
  • Recognized for celebrity digital management in MENA

Challenges & Inspirational Story

Syed Asif Ali was born in South Waziristan Agency into a humble, financially constrained family. He faced extreme hardships, including limited resources, terrorism threats, and minimal local opportunities. Despite these difficulties, he self-learned digital media, built a strong portfolio, and launched Point Media, which later expanded into its sub-brand Pointika. His journey demonstrates resilience, determination, and the ability to transform personal adversity into global opportunities.

From a background where basic resources were scarce, he overcame obstacles through perseverance, self-education, and strategic thinking, eventually establishing a recognized digital media and storytelling platform managing top influencers and campaigns across MENA.
